Detailed Description

The KineticClassifier class implements a baysian classifier that determine the methylation state of CpGs from PacBio CCS data. The classifier implements a 2 class model (methylated and unmethylated) and assigns a probability of belonging to each class, for each CpG, given the kinetics of the CCS mapping over the CpG region.

Computes the posterior probabilities p(methylated | IPD,PWD) and p(unmethylated | IPD,PWD) for the given region, given the CCS read kinetics. The prior class probabilites p(methylated) and p(unmethylated) are normalized to sum up to 1.

Parameters
cpgthe coordinates of a CpG to classify, in genome coordinates. The CpG on both strand will be considered. The coordinates must be strandless. Start must be correspond to the coordinate of the C on the + strand and end to passed the G coordinate on the + strand.
ccssa list of CCS reads mapping on the CpG of interest. Their IPD and PWD kinetics will be used to compute the probability that the CpG is methylated or not methylated. CCS not mapping over the CpG or without a perfect alignment will be ignored.
prob_meththe a priori probability of the methylated class.
prob_unmeththe a priori probability of the unmethylated class.
Returns
a pair of posterior probability p(methylated | IPD,PWD) and p(unmethylated | IPD,PWD) respectively. The probabilities sum to 1.
